My Hobbies

One of my biggest passions is building things. Whenever I come across an interesting object or device, I always wonder how it works and how it was put together. And once I figure it out, I love to try building it myself.

I'm a big fan of DIY projects and enjoy building things from scratch. Some of my favorite projects include building custom keyboards, my phone, my pc, making my own furniture, apps and websites. One of my most recent and exciting projects was building a custom smart mirror, which i'm still working on it (the problem with DIY is that the pieces you need the most are the ones that take the longest to arrive XD).

I also enjoy learning new skills and techniques for building things. I'm always looking for new challenges and projects to work on. One of my long-term goals is to build my own smart home with all my automation system built by me using Raspberry Pi and other open-source tools.

Overall, building things is a huge part of my life and brings me a lot of joy and satisfaction. I love the feeling of creating something with my own hands and seeing it come to life. It's a hobby that allows me to express my creativity and constantly challenge myself to learn new things.
